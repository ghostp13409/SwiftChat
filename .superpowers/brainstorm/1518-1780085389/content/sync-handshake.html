Gossip Protocol: Sync Handshake

How two devices synchronize messages when they meet

Handshake Flow
Device A
DB: [m1, m2, m3]
↔
Device B
DB: [m1, m4]
1. Advertise State

Each device sends a summary (hashes/timestamps) of their current message store.

2. Identify Gaps

A sees B needs [m2, m3]. B sees A needs [m4].

3. Data Exchange

A sends [m2, m3] to B. B sends [m4] to A.

Synchronization Strategy

A

Full Hash List

Send IDs and timestamps of ALL active messages. Simple and reliable for smaller message counts (e.g., < 1000 messages).

B

Bloom Filter / IBLT

Send a probabilistic summary. More efficient for massive message counts, but significantly more complex to implement and debug.
