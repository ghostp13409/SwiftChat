Gossip Engine: Sync Frequency

How often and when devices should sync with peers

Recommended

Smart Sync

Scan periodically (every 2-5 mins) and keep a "Last Synced" list per peer. Avoids redundant work while maintaining a fresh mesh.

  • Balanced battery
  • Fast propagation

Active Sync

Constant scanning and immediate connection to any new peer found. Max speed, but high battery drain.

  • High battery usage
  • Constant background activity

Passive Sync

Only sync when the user opens the app or is already connected for a direct chat. Lowest battery impact.

  • Slow message delivery
  • Weak mesh reliability
