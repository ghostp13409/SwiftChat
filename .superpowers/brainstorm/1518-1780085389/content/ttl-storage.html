TTL & Storage Limits

How messages expire and how we manage local cache

Configuration Settings
Message Expiry (TTL)
24 Hours (Default)

Options: 1h, 4h, 8h, 24h, 1w, 4w

Messages are automatically purged after this duration from the time they were received.

Relay Cache Limit
100 MB

Oldest relay packets are purged if limit is exceeded.

This applies only to messages being relayed for others, not your own chats.

Automatic Cleanup Strategy

A

Periodic Timer

Run a cleanup task every 15-30 minutes while the app is in the foreground.

B

On-Sync Cleanup

Clean up expired messages just before or after a new mesh sync session starts.
