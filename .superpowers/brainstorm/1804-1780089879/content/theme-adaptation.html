Design Language: Thick Borders & Depth

In both modes, we use "physical" elements (thick borders and offset shadows) to make buttons and cards feel touchable.

A

Neubrutalist (High Contrast)

Keep the black/white borders in both modes. Very bold, very Transit-app style. Max clarity.

B

Soft Organic (Lower Contrast)

Use colored borders/shadows instead of black/white. Feels "softer" and more modern/premium, but slightly less glanceable.
