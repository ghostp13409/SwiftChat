Information Architecture: Navigation Patterns

How should users move between the main sections of the app?

Traditional

Classic Bottom Nav

3 distinct tabs: RADAR, CHATS, and PROFILE. Best for multi-tasking and familiarity.

RADARCHATSPROFILE
Utility-First

Radar-Centric

The Radar is the HOME. Swipe up for active chats, or tap a corner for profile. Maximizes the "discovery" experience.

RADAR
Premium

The Slide-Deck

One unified screen. Swipe left for PROFILE, swipe right for CHATS. Radar stays in the middle. Feels very high-end and fluid.

Structural Decision #1

Pick the pattern that serves the "Low Cognitive Load" philosophy best.
