# SwiftChat Overview

SwiftChat is a p2p local chat app, designed to work without an internet connection or a cellular network. It will utilize Bluetooth and Wi-FI or a combination of both to create a mesh network, allowing users to communicate with each other even in areas with limited connectivity. This app will be mainly focused on providing a seamless and secure communication while traveling. The app will be built using flutter.

## Features

- **Mesh Networking**: The app will work on a similar protocol as Quick Share and Airdrop, utilizing Bluetooth and Wi-Fi to create a mesh network, allowing users to communicate even in areas with limited connectivity.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: SwiftChat will have a simple and intuitive interface, making it easy for users to navigate and use the app. Some user inspiration can be taken from apps like transit app and celtestpip website.
- **Secure Communication**: The app will prioritize user privacy and security, ensuring that all communications are encrypted and protected from unauthorized access.
- **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: SwiftChat will be built using Flutter, allowing it to run on both iOS and Android devices, ensuring that users can communicate regardless of their device type.
- **Temporary Chats**: the chats would be completely temporary and would be deleted after a certain period of time, ensuring that users' conversations are not stored indefinitely. This feature will enhance privacy and security, as users can have confidence that their conversations will not be accessible after a certain period of time.
- **Group Chats**: SwiftChat will also support group chats, allowing users to communicate with multiple people at once. This feature will be particularly useful for travelers who are in a group and want to stay connected without relying on an internet connection.
- **Local Only**: SwiftChat would be offline only giving users the confidence that their conversations are not being stored or transmitted over the internet. This feature will enhance privacy and security, as users can have confidence that their conversations are not accessible to third parties.
- **File Sharing**: Users will be able to share files, photos, and other media with each other through the app, making it a versatile communication tool for travelers.
- **Visibility**: Users will have an option to set their visibility to either contacts only or public, allowing them to control who can see their presence on the app and communicate with them. This feature will enhance privacy and security, as users can choose to only be visible to their contacts, reducing the risk of unwanted communication from strangers.
- **Profiles**: Users will be able to create profiles with their name(optional), photo(optional), username, and a short bio. This will allow users to personalize their experience and make it easier for others to identify them on the app. Additionally, users can also add thier topics of interest to their profile, which can help them connect with like-minded individuals and find relevant conversations on the app. This feature will enhance the social aspect of the app and make it more engaging for users.
- **WiFi Direct Package**: The app will utilize a WiFi Direct package to enable direct communication between devices without the need for an internet connection. This will allow users to communicate with each other even in areas with limited connectivity, making it an ideal solution for travelers who may not have access to a reliable internet connection. The WiFi Direct package will also enhance the performance of the app, allowing for faster communication and file sharing between devices.
- **End-to-End Encryption**: The app will implement end-to-end encryption to ensure that all communications are secure and protected from unauthorized access.
- **Diffie-Hellman Key Exchange**: The app will implement the Diffie-Hellman key exchange algorithm to establish a secure communication channel between users. This algorithm allows users to securely exchange cryptographic keys over an insecure communication channel, ensuring that their conversations are protected from unauthorized access.
